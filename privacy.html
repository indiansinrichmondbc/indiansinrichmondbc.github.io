Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 28, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how personal information is collected, used, and protected when you use the Indians in Richmond, BC website ("the Site").

Information We Collect

We do not actively collect personal information about visitors. If you choose to contact us, join our WhatsApp group, or participate in our social media groups, your information may be subject to those platforms' privacy policies.

Third-Party Services & Facebook

Our site links to external sites and uses third-party services, including StatCounter for analytics and social media platforms such as Facebook. Your activity on Facebook, including joining our group or interacting with Facebook content, is governed by Facebook's Privacy Policy and not this site. We do not control external providers and encourage you to review their privacy policies.

How We Use Information

Any information you choose to share with us (for example, by submitting a business listing) is used solely for community purposes and will not be sold or rented to third parties.

Cookies

The Site itself does not set first-party cookies. Third-party services (such as analytics providers or social platforms) may set cookies according to their own policies.

Security

We take reasonable steps to protect your information, but no method of transmission over the internet is completely secure. Please use discretion when sharing personal information.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy at any time. Changes will be posted on this page with a revised date.

Contact

For questions about this policy, please contact the site administrators via our Facebook group.

For more information about how Facebook handles your data, please read the Facebook Privacy Policy.
